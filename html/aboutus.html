Our Story

From Garage to Global

Founded in 2015, Drop The Beat began as a small collective of music enthusiasts in a garage studio. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a thriving community of artists, producers, and music lovers.

Today, we're proud to be one of the leading platforms for discovering emerging talent and connecting artists with their audiences worldwide. Our team spans across 15 countries, united by our love for music.

We've hosted over 500 events, worked with 2000+ artists, and reached millions of music lovers through our digital platforms. But our journey is just beginning.
